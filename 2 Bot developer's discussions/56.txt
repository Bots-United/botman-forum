Edit the bot.cfg file using Notepad.  Change the "botskill 2" to "botskill 3" or "botskill 4" or "botskill 4".

Skill level 2 (the default) is very good aim and very quick.  Skill level 3, 4 or 5 is slower and worse aim.  Skill level 1 is the best, skill level 5 is the worst.
